about rd

Randen Engineering Pvt. Ltd., formerly known as RD Engineering, is a mechanical engineering firm and a part of a group involved in undertaking Engineering Procurement and Construction (EPC) projects for Structures, Piping, Tankage, Equipment Fabrication and Erection across India and overseas for the last 40 years. We provide customized and innovative solutions for all your unique requirements, and hence, are a complete integrated solution provider. With headquarters in Mumbai, India, we deal with heavy fabrication facilities in India and with capabilities backed by past experience,we execute mechanical engineering projects in Mozambique, Ethiopia, Ghana and South Africa. In order to carry out site jobs of erection and installation, we have a large pool of cranes and other equipments to undertake any project across the country as well as globally. We are associated with Don Bosco, Pinguli, Maharashtra to recruit well-qualified technicians as well as to provide industrial training to our candidates.
